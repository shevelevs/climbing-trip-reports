# Aliona

Aliona is an all-around climber and mountaineer who equally thrives on technical rock routes and high-altitude alpine objectives. Her passion for the mountains ranges from hard sport climbing to technical multi-pitch lines in many premier destinations all around the US—including Red Rocks, Owens River Gorge, and Smith Rock—as well as across Europe in France, Italy, and Spain.

She is no stranger to grueling endurance challenges and sufferfests, having completed single-push endeavors like the Presidential Traverse in New Hampshire. Her mountaineering resume includes Mt Rainier via Liberty Ridge as well as demanding high-altitude expeditions like Denali's West Buttress route.
