# Aliona

Aliona is an all-around climber and mountaineer who equally thrives on technical rock routes and high-altitude alpine objectives. Her passion for the mountains ranges from technical multi-pitch lines in the High Sierra, like the East Buttress of Mt Whitney, to hard sport climbing in premier destinations all around us—including Red Rocks, Owens River Gorge, and Smith Rock—as well as across Europe in France, Italy, and Spain.

She is no stranger to grueling endurance challenges and sufferfests, having completed single-push endeavors like the Presidential Traverse in New Hampshire. Her mountaineering resume includes summiting Mt Rainier in Washington and participating in long, demanding high-altitude expeditions, including climbing Denali's West Buttress route in Alaska.
